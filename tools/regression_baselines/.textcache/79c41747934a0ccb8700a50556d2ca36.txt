===== Admission Bribery / Education Payment Routing =====
Look for structured payments from many students or parents, convergence into education-service accounts, and cash withdrawal after collections.

===== Hawala Operation =====
Look for matched debit-credit pairs with 2-3 percent commission difference within 24-48 hours.

===== Mule Chain / Layering =====
Look for near-zero dwell time, multi-hop chains, and convergence into terminal accounts.

===== Government Scheme Fraud =====
Look for multiple beneficiary names mapping to the same account number and APBS/MGNREGA-style credits.

===== Cyber Fraud / OTP Scam =====
Look for velocity spikes, synchronized transactions, and many new counterparties.

===== Blind Audit =====
Enter no case brief. The system should infer the likely crime pattern from hard-coded findings.
